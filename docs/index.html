    Artists

    • Hey Ocean! has been a big part of my life since I was a teenager. David Vertesi, Ashleigh Ball, and David Beckingham make music that feels like love and comfort. I discovered them through Ashleigh Ball and have been hooked ever since. Their songs aren’t just nostalgic—they bring me joy, peace, and a sense of who I am. This band has truly shaped me and will always have a special place in my heart.

    • Daniel Ingram is a phenomenal artist whose music has worked and inspired the My Little Pony community.     • Crywank is an English anti-folk band based out of Manchester, originally started by a single musician, Jay Clayton, pouring pure feeling into his work. The band’s music blends love, despair, and raw honesty, cutting through with an authenticity that is both beautiful and haunting. Jay carved a unique place for Crywank, creating songs that resonate deeply with listeners. The impact of this band runs so profound that some fans have even been buried wearing Crywank t-shirts. Harrowing as it is, a testament to the powerful connection people feel with the music.

    • Oasis feels like a natural part of life, like they’re woven into my culture itself. I discovered them through my best mates, and from that moment, their music became something more than just songs; it’s ethereal, like it exists on another plane entirely. There’s an unexplainable magic to their sound, the way it can be massive and raw yet somehow deeply personal at the same time. Oasis isn’t just a band—they’re an experience, and for me, they’re nothing short of amazing.
