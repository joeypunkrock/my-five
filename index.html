    • Crywank is an English anti-folk band based out of Manchester, originally started by Jay Clayton, pouring pure feeling into his work. The band’s music blends love, despair, and raw honesty, cutting through with an authenticity that is both beautiful and haunting. Jay creates songs that resonate deeply with people. The impact of this band runs so profound that some fans have even been buried wearing Crywank t-shirts. Harrowing as it is, a testament to the powerful connection people feel with the music.
      Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GiesKu55IFk
